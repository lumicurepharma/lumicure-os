# LKS-002 Charter

# Business Architecture of Lumicure

## Purpose

LKS-002 shall define how Lumicure creates, delivers, protects, and scales value as a business.

It shall translate the Constitution and Enterprise Architecture into a clear business design.

It shall serve as the foundation for:

* DPR preparation
* Bank discussions
* Investor materials
* Export strategy
* Operating model
* Financial architecture
* Golilo product strategy
* First employee understanding
* AI agent context

---

## Core Question

LKS-002 shall answer one question:

**How does Lumicure create, deliver, protect, and scale value?**

---

## Scope

LKS-002 shall cover:

1. Business identity
2. Business thesis
3. First principles
4. Value creation model
5. Merchant export model
6. Golilo evolution model
7. Customer segments
8. Manufacturer segments
9. Product scope
10. Geographic strategy
11. Revenue architecture
12. Cost architecture
13. Risk-controlled growth model
14. Lumicure to LKS to LOS to Golilo learning loop
15. Government scheme alignment
16. Key design decisions

---

## Boundaries

LKS-002 shall not define:

* Detailed SOPs
* Full financial projections
* Technology stack
* Full Golilo product requirements
* Complete ontology
* Full export documentation manual
* Detailed HR structure
* Complete regulatory manual

Those shall be handled in specialized LKS documents.

## Governing Principles

LKS-002 shall follow these principles:

Truth before trust.

Trust before growth.

Business reality before theory.

Merchant export before marketplace.

Validated operations before technology.

Profit with compliance.

Growth with discipline.

Knowledge before software.

Golilo shall emerge from Lumicure’s validated learning.

---

## Key Design Decisions to Capture

LKS-002 must explicitly capture the following design decisions:

1. Lumicure begins as a merchant exporter.

2. Lumicure will not manufacture during Phase 1.

3. Golilo is not the initial revenue engine.

4. Merchant export operations are Customer Zero.

5. Trust precedes scale.

6. Technology follows validated operations.

7. Business architecture must support DPR, bankability, operations, and future platform design.

---

## Completion Criteria

LKS-002 shall be considered complete when it clearly explains:

1. What business Lumicure is in today.

2. What business Lumicure is building toward.

3. Who Lumicure serves.

4. What value Lumicure creates.

5. How Lumicure earns revenue across phases.

6. How Lumicure controls risk.

7. How Lumicure’s export operations create knowledge for Golilo.

8. What future documents depend on this business architecture.

---

## Approval Rule

LKS-002 shall remain Draft until reviewed against:

* LKS-000 Constitution
* LKS-001 Enterprise Architecture
* ADR-0001

After approval, later documents shall reference LKS-002 rather than redefine Lumicure’s business model.
