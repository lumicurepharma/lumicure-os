# LKS-003 Charter

# Operating Model of Lumicure

## Purpose

LKS-003 shall define how Lumicure converts its approved Business Architecture into executable operating structure.

It shall translate LKS-002 into roles, workflows, responsibilities, authority, review cycles, systems, and operating discipline.

## Core Question

LKS-003 shall answer one question:

**Given this architecture, who does what, when, how, with what authority, and using which systems?**

## Scope

LKS-003 shall cover:

1. Operating model purpose
2. Core operating principles
3. Roles and responsibilities
4. Authority and escalation
5. Operating cadence
6. Workflow architecture
7. Knowledge capture responsibilities
8. Decision and review loops
9. Interface with LKS, LOS, and future SOPs
10. Operating implications for export execution, finance, trust, technology, and AI

## Boundaries

LKS-003 shall not define:

* Detailed SOPs
* Full HR policy
* Complete approval matrix
* Financial model
* Export documentation manual
* Technology stack
* AI agent design

Those shall be handled in specialized LKS documents and SOPs.

## Completion Criteria

LKS-003 shall be considered complete when it clearly explains:

1. How Lumicure's work is organized.
2. Who owns major operating responsibilities.
3. How decisions move through the organization.
4. How knowledge is captured from execution.
5. How workflows connect to SOPs.
6. How operating discipline protects trust and compliance.
7. How the operating model supports LKS-002.

## Approval Rule

LKS-003 shall remain Draft until reviewed against:

* LKS-000 Constitution of Lumicure
* LKS-001 Enterprise Architecture of Lumicure
* LKS-002 Business Architecture of Lumicure
* ADR-0001
* ADR-0002
* LKS Quality Standard v1.0

After approval, later SOPs, workflows, hiring plans, and operating systems shall reference LKS-003 rather than redefine Lumicure's operating model.
