# ADR-0003

# Operating Model Must Be Executable and Founder-Independent

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Lumicure is beginning Sprint 4 to transform the approved Business Architecture into an executable Operating Model.

The Operating Model must not become theoretical, impressive, or dependent on the Founder being present for every decision.

LKS-003 must define work in a way that can be executed in real operating conditions and eventually delegated to capable people using approved LKS and LOS documents.

If the Operating Model cannot be executed next Monday morning, it is too abstract.

If the Operating Model cannot eventually be executed without constant Founder presence, it is too dependent on Founder memory.

---

## Decision

The Operating Model shall be designed only for work that can be executed in real operating conditions and eventually performed correctly by capable employees using approved LKS and LOS documents without constant founder presence.

LKS-003 shall therefore be governed by two permanent tests:

1. Rule 004 — Monday Morning Executability
2. Rule 005 — Founder Independence

---

## Rule 004 — Monday Morning Executability

Every Operating Model decision shall pass the Monday Morning Executability Test:

**Can Harshal execute this next Monday morning?**

This test prevents LKS-003 from becoming theoretical.

An Operating Model decision is acceptable only if it can be translated into real work, real decisions, real ownership, real information flow, or real review behavior.

If a section cannot influence execution, delegation, review, learning, or control, it probably does not belong in LKS-003.

---

## Rule 005 — Founder Independence

Every Operating Model decision shall also pass the Founder Independence Test:

**Could a capable new employee execute this correctly without Harshal being present, using only the approved LKS and LOS documents?**

This test prevents Lumicure from becoming dependent on Founder memory.

If the answer is no, then one of three things is missing:

* the Operating Model,
* an SOP,
* or institutional knowledge.

Founder involvement may be necessary in the early phase.

Founder dependence must not become the long-term operating design.

---

## Rationale

This decision exists to:

1. Keep LKS-003 grounded in real work.

2. Prevent theoretical operating architecture.

3. Force clarity around ownership, triggers, outcomes, authority, escalation, and knowledge capture.

4. Support delegation without losing accountability.

5. Reduce dependence on Founder memory.

6. Prepare Lumicure to become an institution rather than a Founder-dependent business.

---

## Consequences

This decision will result in:

1. A more practical Operating Model.

2. Clearer requirements for SOPs, workflows, roles, and review cadence.

3. Better founder independence over time.

4. Stronger institutional memory.

5. Better ability to train future employees.

6. Better alignment between LKS, LOS, AI agents, and real execution.

---

## Decision Principle

Operating design must become executable work.

Operating design must eventually support capable execution without constant Founder presence.

---

## Review

This ADR shall govern Sprint 4 and may be reviewed after LKS-003 Operating Model reaches Approved status.
