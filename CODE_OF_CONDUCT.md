## Standard

Lumicure contributors should work with clarity, respect, confidentiality, and accountability.

## Expectations

1. Protect sensitive company, customer, patient, partner, and research information.
2. Challenge ideas directly while treating people professionally.
3. Record decisions, assumptions, and risks instead of hiding them in private conversations.
4. Keep LOS useful for future contributors and AI agents.
5. Do not use AI-generated material as strategic authority unless the decision is accepted into LOS.
