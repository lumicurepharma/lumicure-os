## Contribution Rules

LOS is maintained like software.

Before making changes:

1. Read the top-level README.
2. Read the relevant module README.
3. Check dependencies, related chapters, and decision history.
4. Decide whether the change needs a decision log, RFC, or ADR.

## Metadata

Every page must include:

- Title
- Version
- Status
- Owner
- Last Updated
- Dependencies
- Related Chapters
- Review Date
- References
- Decision History

## Change Types

- Patch changes correct or clarify existing content.
- Minor changes add a new capability, framework, chapter, or accepted process.
- Major changes alter the architecture, governance, or strategic foundation of LOS.

## AI Contributions

AI may draft, format, search, summarize, and implement.

AI must not decide Lumicure strategy. Strategic decisions must be accepted inside LOS before AI implements them.
