## Decision

Use `Project Axiom` as the internal codename for the foundational buildout of LOS.

## Reason

An axiom is a fundamental truth from which other truths are derived. This matches the role of LOS as the constitutional and organizational foundation for Lumicure.

## Alternatives

- Continue using only LOS v1.0
- Use a product-style codename
- Use no codename

## Accepted Direction

Use `Project Axiom` internally while keeping `Lumicure Organizational System` and `LOS` as the formal names.
