## Context

LOS needs stable navigation for humans and AI agents.

## Decision

Use numbered top-level modules from `00_CONSTITUTION` through `16_APPENDIX`.

## Alternatives

- Unnumbered topical folders
- Date-based folders
- Single document hierarchy

## Consequences

Module order becomes stable, references become easier to parse, and AI agents can load context predictably.
